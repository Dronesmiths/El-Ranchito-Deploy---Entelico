Care Tips for Longer-Lasting Flowers

  1. Place flowers away from direct sun and hot windows.
  2. Refresh the water regularly and keep the vase clean.
  3. Trim stems at an angle every few days.
  4. Remove any leaves sitting below the waterline.
  5. Keep arrangements away from appliances that release heat.
