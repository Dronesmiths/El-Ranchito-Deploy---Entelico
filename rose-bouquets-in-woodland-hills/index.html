How to Get the Best Result from Your Bouquet

Once your roses arrive, a few simple steps can help them last longer and look their best.

Unwrap carefully

Remove packaging gently so petals are not bent or bruised. If the bouquet includes a water source, keep it upright while preparing a vase.

Use a clean vase

A freshly washed vase helps reduce bacteria buildup, which can shorten the life of roses. Fill it with cool water and flower food if provided.

Trim the stems

Cut stems at an angle before placing them in water. This improves water absorption and helps the roses stay hydrated.

Keep them cool

In Woodland Hills, avoid placing roses in direct afternoon sun, near appliances, or close to warm windows. A cooler indoor spot helps preserve the bouquet’s freshness.
