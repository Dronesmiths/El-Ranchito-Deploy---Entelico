What to Write on a Sympathy Card

The message that accompanies sympathy flowers can be just as meaningful as the arrangement itself. If you are struggling with what to say, keep the note simple, sincere, and personal. You do not need elaborate language. In fact, the most comforting messages are often the most direct.

  • “With deepest sympathy during this difficult time.”
  • “Thinking of you and sending love and comfort.”
  • “May these flowers bring a moment of peace and remembrance.”
  • “Holding you and your family in our hearts.”
  • “With heartfelt condolences from all of us at Flowers El Ranchito.”

If the arrangement is being sent to a Burbank home near Chandler Bikeway, a studio office off Olive, or a church near downtown Burbank, a brief handwritten note can make the gesture feel deeply personal.
