How to Choose the Right Wedding Flowers for Your Event

If you are early in the planning process, the best way to choose wedding flowers is to start with the overall feeling you want your guests to experience. Then work backward into color, flower type, and arrangement scale.

  1. Start with your venue. An outdoor garden wedding may call for softer, more natural arrangements, while a formal indoor venue may benefit from structured designs.
  2. Choose a color palette. Your flowers should complement your attire, linens, and decor rather than compete with them.
  3. Decide where flowers matter most. Some couples prioritize the bouquet and ceremony arch, while others focus on reception tables and entry pieces.
  4. Consider weather and timing. Valley Village temperatures and event start times can influence flower selection and delivery strategy.
  5. Match your style to your budget. A thoughtful design with the right focal pieces can look luxurious without unnecessary excess.
