Orchid Care Tips for Burbank Customers

Keeping orchids looking their best is easier when you follow a few simple care practices. Since Burbank homes and offices often experience dry indoor air, a little attention goes a long way.

  1. Place orchids in bright, indirect light rather than harsh direct sun.
  2. Avoid overwatering; orchids prefer a careful, measured approach.
  3. Keep arrangements away from heating vents and strong AC blasts.
  4. Rotate the container occasionally so blooms receive even light.
  5. Remove spent blooms gently to maintain a tidy appearance.

With the right care, orchids can remain an elegant part of your space for an extended period, making them one of the most rewarding floral choices available.
