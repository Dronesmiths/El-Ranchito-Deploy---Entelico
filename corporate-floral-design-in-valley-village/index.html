What Makes a Corporate Floral Arrangement Work

Strong corporate floral design is disciplined. It is not about using the most flowers possible. It is about using the right flowers in the right proportions, with the right vessel, height, color, and placement.

Scale and proportion

An arrangement that is too tall can block conversation. One that is too small can disappear in a large lobby. We balance the composition so it supports the space visually and functionally.

Color strategy

In Valley Village, where many businesses favor contemporary interiors and clean finishes, color choice matters. Soft neutrals can create a calm professional feel. Deeper tones can add richness and authority. Bright accents can energize a creative workspace. We tailor color to the room and the business objective.

Container selection

Vases and vessels are part of the design, not an afterthought. Sleek ceramic, glass, metallic, and textured containers each communicate something different. We select materials that align with the business environment and support the flowers structurally.
