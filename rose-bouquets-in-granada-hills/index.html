How to Make Your Rose Bouquet Last Longer

Once your bouquet arrives, a few simple steps can help extend its life. Trim stems at an angle, use clean water, keep the arrangement away from direct sun and heat vents, and refresh the water regularly. In a climate like Granada Hills, where warm afternoons can accelerate dehydration, these small habits make a noticeable difference.

Simple Care Tips

  1. Place the bouquet in fresh water as soon as possible.
  2. Use a clean vase to reduce bacteria buildup.
  3. Keep roses away from fruit bowls and direct sunlight.
  4. Remove wilted petals gently to preserve the overall look.
  5. Re-cut stems every couple of days for better water uptake.
