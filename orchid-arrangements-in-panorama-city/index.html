Orchid Care Tips for Recipients

Orchids are admired for their longevity, but a little care helps them stay fresh even longer.

Keep Them Out of Direct Sun

Panorama City sunshine can be intense, especially through windows that face west or south. Orchids do best in bright but indirect light.

Avoid Overwatering

One of the most common mistakes is giving orchids too much water. A modest watering schedule is usually best, depending on the arrangement style and container.

Place Them Away from Heat Sources

Keep orchids away from vents, stovetops, and hot appliances. In the Valley’s dry climate, stable indoor conditions help preserve bloom quality.
