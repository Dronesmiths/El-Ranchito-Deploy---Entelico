Seasonal Flower Choices for Southern California Weddings

Seasonality is important, even in a climate as flower-friendly as Los Angeles County. Some blooms hold up better in warm weather, while others are best used in controlled indoor environments. We help couples choose flowers that fit both their vision and the season.

Common seasonal favorites

  • Spring: peonies, ranunculus, garden roses, sweet peas
  • Summer: roses, orchids, lisianthus, hydrangeas, calla lilies
  • Fall: dahlias, chrysanthemums, roses, carnations, textured greenery
  • Winter: white roses, amaryllis, orchids, evergreen accents, anemones

If your wedding takes place during a hotter stretch of the year, we may recommend designs with fewer fragile petals and more structure. If the event is in a cooler season or fully indoors, we can expand the range of delicate and luxurious blooms. The result is a wedding floral plan that looks elevated and stays dependable.
