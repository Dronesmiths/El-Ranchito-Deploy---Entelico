Care Tips for Keeping Rose Bouquets Fresh Longer

Once your roses arrive, a little care goes a long way. Glendale’s dry climate means bouquets benefit from prompt attention and proper hydration.

  1. Trim the stems at an angle before placing the bouquet in water.
  2. Use a clean vase with fresh water.
  3. Keep the bouquet away from direct sun, vents, and heat-producing appliances.
  4. Change the water regularly to help the roses last longer.
  5. Remove any leaves that sit below the waterline.

These small steps help preserve the bouquet’s appearance and extend enjoyment, especially during warmer Glendale days.
