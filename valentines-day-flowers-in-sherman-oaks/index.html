Delivery Tips for Valentine’s Day in Sherman Oaks

Because Valentine’s Day is one of the busiest floral delivery days of the year, a little planning goes a long way. The Sherman Oaks area includes apartment buildings, hillside homes, office suites, and restaurant reservations that all have different access needs. Here are a few ways to make delivery smoother.

  1. Provide complete address details. Include apartment numbers, gate codes, suite numbers, and any landmark-based instructions if the entrance is hard to find.
  2. Choose a realistic delivery window. Morning and early afternoon deliveries often work best when trying to avoid traffic near the 101/405 corridor.
  3. Tell us about the recipient’s routine. If they work from home, commute, or attend a dinner reservation, that helps us plan the ideal drop-off time.
  4. Keep the message personal. A handwritten card message elevates the gift and makes the bouquet feel more intimate.
  5. Order early. Valentine’s Day demand in Sherman Oaks rises quickly, and early ordering helps secure the best stems and preferred delivery timing.
