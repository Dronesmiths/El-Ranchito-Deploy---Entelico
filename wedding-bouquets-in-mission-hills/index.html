Flowers That Perform Beautifully in Mission Hills

Choosing the right flowers is essential, especially when weddings take place in warmer inland conditions or during months with stronger afternoon sun. Mission Hills can experience dry heat, bright light, and occasional marine influence depending on the season and time of day, so we select blooms accordingly.

Popular bouquet flowers we recommend

  1. Roses: Timeless, versatile, and available in countless shades for romantic palettes.
  2. Ranunculus: A favorite for layered texture and soft, elegant movement.
  3. Peonies: Ideal for lush spring weddings with a luxurious feel.
  4. Orchids: Excellent for modern or upscale designs and strong visual impact.
