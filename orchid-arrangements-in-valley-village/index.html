Simple Care Tips That Help Orchids Last Longer

  1. Keep orchids away from direct sunlight, especially windows that face strong afternoon heat.
  2. Avoid placing arrangements directly under vents or near heating and cooling units.
  3. Use clean water and follow the care instructions provided with the arrangement.
  4. Choose a stable location away from heavy foot traffic, pets, and kitchen heat.
  5. If the arrangement is in a decorative container, check that it drains or is maintained properly.

With these basics, your orchid arrangement can remain a beautiful feature in your home or business for an extended period. If you are unsure how to care for your specific design, Flowers El Ranchito can guide you with practical, easy-to-follow recommendations.
