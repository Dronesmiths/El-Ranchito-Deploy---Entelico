Then consider the personality of the quinceañera: Is she drawn to soft and romantic styles, or does she want something bold and glamorous? Once those choices are clear, the flowers can be selected to support the overall vision.

Consider the season

Seasonal flowers often provide the best value and availability. They also tend to perform better in Southern California conditions. We can recommend blooms that suit spring, summer, fall, or winter events while still matching the desired color palette.

Think about the venue lighting

Indoor venues with warm lighting may benefit from brighter flowers and crisp whites, while shaded church interiors may call for richer tones and more contrast. Outdoor venues in Northridge require sturdy designs that can withstand sun exposure and light wind.

Match scale to the space
