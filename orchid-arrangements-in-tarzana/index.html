How to Care for Orchid Arrangements

One of the best things about orchid arrangements is that they are relatively simple to maintain when you know the basics. At Flowers El Ranchito, we always want our customers to enjoy their flowers for as long as possible, so we recommend a few simple care steps.

Keep Them in Bright, Indirect Light

Orchids do best in bright but filtered light. In Tarzana homes with large windows, it is usually best to keep them away from direct afternoon sun, especially during hotter months. Too much sun can cause blooms to fade or dry out more quickly.

Water Sparingly

Overwatering is one of the most common orchid mistakes. Most orchid arrangements need only modest watering, and the exact schedule depends on the container and the type of orchid. The goal is to keep roots healthy without letting them sit in excess moisture.

Avoid Heat Vents and Drafts

Because Tarzana can swing between warm days and cooler evenings, orchids should be kept away from vents, heaters, and strong drafts. Stable indoor conditions help the blooms last longer and keep the plant looking healthy.
