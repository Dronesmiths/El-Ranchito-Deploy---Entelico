Types of Event Floral Decorations We Create

Every occasion calls for a different floral strategy. Some events need dramatic installations, while others benefit from subtle elegance. Below are some of the most popular floral décor services we provide for Sherman Oaks clients.

Centerpieces and table arrangements

Table florals are the foundation of many events. We design centerpieces that complement the scale of your tables, the formality of the event, and the visibility needed for conversation. For long banquet tables, we may use low, continuous arrangements. For round tables, we may create fuller focal pieces that anchor the setting without feeling crowded.

Ceremony and entrance florals

For weddings, vow renewals, and formal celebrations, entrance décor and ceremony florals create the first emotional moment. Think aisle markers, arch florals, welcome arrangements, and focal pieces that frame the couple or speaker beautifully. In Sherman Oaks, where many events happen in homes with courtyards or garden-adjacent spaces, these designs often bridge indoor elegance with outdoor freshness.

Stage and podium florals

For corporate functions, fundraisers, and community events, stage florals help define the visual center of the room. Podium arrangements should be visible on camera, balanced in scale, and designed to avoid obstructing speakers or signage. We often recommend arrangements that work well under bright event lighting and still read clearly in photos and video.

Reception and lounge styling

Florals can also support cocktail areas, lounge vignettes, bars, dessert stations, and gift tables. These smaller touchpoints help create a cohesive atmosphere across the entire event. A well-placed arrangement at a bar or entry console can make a strong impression even before guests reach their seats.
