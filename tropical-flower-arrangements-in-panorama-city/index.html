Care Tips for Tropical Flower Arrangements

To get the most from your tropical arrangement, a few simple care steps can help extend its beauty:

  • Keep the arrangement away from direct hot sun when possible.
  • Place it in a cool indoor area with moderate airflow.
  • Refresh water as instructed and keep the container clean.
  • Remove any fading foliage to maintain a tidy appearance.
  • Avoid setting it near vents, ovens, or appliances that release heat.

These tips are especially helpful in Panorama City, where dry heat and strong afternoon exposure can shorten the life of any floral design if it is not positioned carefully.
