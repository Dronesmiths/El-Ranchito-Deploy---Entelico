Freshness, Longevity, and Floral Care

The best bouquet is one that keeps its beauty after delivery. That is especially important in Tarzana, where warm afternoons can accelerate wilting if flowers are not handled correctly. We use careful conditioning, proper hydration, and balanced stem selection to help extend vase life.

For customers, a few simple care steps can make a big difference:

  1. Trim stems at an angle when placing flowers in water.
  2. Use a clean vase and fresh water.
  3. Keep the bouquet away from direct sun and heat vents.
  4. Remove any leaves below the waterline.
  5. Refresh the water every couple of days.

These small habits help your custom bouquet stay vibrant longer, whether it is displayed in a Tarzana kitchen, office lobby, or dining room.
