Seasonality, Longevity, and Floral Care in the San Fernando Valley

Because Chatsworth experiences dry heat and strong sunlight for much of the year, flower selection and care are essential. Luxury floral design must account for local climate challenges so arrangements remain fresh, vibrant, and structurally sound.

Best Practices for Longevity

  • Use conditioned stems and clean vessels for maximum freshness.
  • Choose blooms that tolerate temperature changes when needed.
  • Place arrangements away from direct sunlight and vents.
  • Refresh water regularly and trim stems as recommended.
  • For events, time delivery as close as possible to setup.

These details may seem small, but they make a major difference in a valley climate. A luxury arrangement should look pristine throughout the day, whether it is displayed in a shaded backyard in Chatsworth or under bright indoor lighting in a corporate office.
