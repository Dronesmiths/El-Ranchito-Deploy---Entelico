Funeral Arrangement Options for Mission Hills Families

Every service is different, and the right tribute depends on the venue, family preferences, and the relationship being honored. Below are some of the most requested funeral floral designs.

Standing Sprays

Standing sprays are one of the most traditional funeral arrangements. Displayed on easels, they create a visible tribute at the service and are often placed near the casket or memorial area. These can be designed in all-white styles or with meaningful color accents.

Casket Sprays

Casket sprays are large floral pieces placed directly on the casket. They are typically chosen by immediate family and can be customized to reflect a loved one’s favorite flowers, colors, or personality.

Sympathy Wreaths

Wreaths symbolize eternal life and continuity, making them a thoughtful choice for funeral homes, churches, and celebration-of-life services. Their circular shape communicates both unity and remembrance.

Floral Baskets and Table Pieces

Smaller arrangements are often ideal for guest books, reception tables, memory tables, or home memorials. These pieces bring warmth without overwhelming the setting.

Sympathy Bouquets for the Home

Not every tribute is meant for the service itself. Many Mission Hills families send sympathy bouquets directly to the home of the bereaved, offering comfort during the days and weeks after the ceremony.
