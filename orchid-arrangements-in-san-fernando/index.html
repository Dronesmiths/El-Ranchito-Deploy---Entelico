How to Care for Orchid Arrangements

One of the biggest advantages of orchids is that they can remain beautiful for an impressive amount of time when cared for properly. If your orchid arrangement is placed in a home near the hilly edges of the Valley, an office along Brand Boulevard connections, or a storefront with bright filtered light, proper care helps preserve the arrangement’s beauty.

Simple orchid care tips

  1. Keep them in indirect light: Orchids love brightness, but direct sun can damage the blooms.
  2. Avoid overwatering: Too much water is one of the most common mistakes with orchids.
  3. Maintain stable temperatures: Orchids prefer moderate indoor conditions rather than hot drafts or cold blasts.
  4. Keep them away from vents: Air conditioning and heating vents can dry out the plant too quickly.
  5. Rotate occasionally: This helps the arrangement maintain a balanced appearance.

In San Fernando, where summer heat and dry indoor air can be a challenge, placement matters. A windowsill with filtered morning light or a table away from direct HVAC airflow is often ideal. If you are displaying orchids in a business near commuter-heavy roads or in a home with active foot traffic, choose a stable surface so the arrangement remains secure and visually centered.
