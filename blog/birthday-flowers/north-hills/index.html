How to Keep Birthday Flowers Fresh Longer in North Hills

Because North Hills can experience hot, dry conditions, flower care matters. Even the most beautiful bouquet benefits from simple post-delivery steps. If the recipient keeps flowers indoors away from direct sun and air vents, they can stay vibrant longer.

Simple care tips

  • Trim stems at an angle before placing them in water.
  • Use a clean vase and fresh water.
  • Keep flowers away from windows that receive harsh afternoon sun.
  • Remove faded petals and leaves to reduce bacteria.
  • Refresh water regularly, especially in warm weather.

These small steps are especially helpful in Valley neighborhoods where indoor temperatures can rise quickly during the day. Good care extends the life of the bouquet and keeps the birthday sentiment visible longer.
