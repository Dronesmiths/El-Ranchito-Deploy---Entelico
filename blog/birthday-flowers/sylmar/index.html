How to Make Birthday Flowers Last Longer

Once the flowers arrive, a little care goes a long way. This is especially important in warm Valley conditions. Keep arrangements away from direct sunlight, vents, and ripening fruit. Refresh the water if the bouquet is in a vase, and trim stems slightly if needed. If the arrangement is hand-tied, move it into water as soon as possible.

For recipients spending time outdoors near a patio, park, or backyard birthday setup, placing the bouquet in a shaded area helps maintain freshness. Even a beautiful arrangement benefits from a cooler spot away from midday sun.
