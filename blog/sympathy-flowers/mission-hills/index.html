FAQ: Sympathy Flowers in Mission Hills

What flowers are most appropriate for sympathy?

White lilies, roses, orchids, carnations, and hydrangea are among the most common and appropriate choices. They convey peace, remembrance, and respect.

Can sympathy flowers be delivered the same day in Mission Hills?

Often, yes. Same-day delivery may be available depending on order timing, flower availability, and the destination within Mission Hills or nearby San Diego neighborhoods.

Should I send flowers to the funeral home or the family’s house?

Either can be appropriate. If there is a public service, funeral home delivery is common. For a more personal gesture, a home delivery can provide comfort after the service.

What should I write on the card?

Keep it brief and sincere. A simple message of sympathy, remembrance, or support is usually best.

Are bright colors appropriate for sympathy flowers?

Sometimes, yes, if they reflect the person’s personality or the family’s wishes. In most cases, soft whites and muted tones are the safest choice.
