How to Make Your Flowers Last Longer at Home

Once the arrangement arrives, a few simple care steps can significantly extend its life. This is especially useful in Sylmar, where indoor temperatures can still be warm depending on the season and the home’s exposure to afternoon sun.

Easy Flower Care Tips

  1. Trim stems at an angle before placing them in water.
  2. Use a clean vase and fresh, cool water.
  3. Keep flowers away from direct sunlight and vents.
  4. Remove any leaves below the waterline.
  5. Refresh the water every one to two days.

These small steps help preserve the bouquet’s shape and color. If the arrangement was designed with durable flowers like roses, chrysanthemums, or orchids, proper care can make it look beautiful for several days longer.
