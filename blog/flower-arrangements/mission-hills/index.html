How to Keep Flower Arrangements Fresh Longer

Once flowers arrive, a few simple care steps can dramatically extend their life. This matters in Mission Hills, where an arrangement may be displayed in a sunny dining room, near a fireplace, or in an entryway that gets afternoon light.

  1. Trim stems at an angle before placing them in water.
  2. Use a clean vase with fresh, cool water.
  3. Remove any leaves below the waterline.
  4. Keep flowers away from direct sun and heating vents.
  5. Refresh water every one to two days.
  6. Recut stems every few days for better hydration.

For arrangements delivered to homes near the canyon rim or along streets with more sun exposure, it helps to place flowers in a cooler indoor location first so they can rehydrate after transit. Good care can add days of beauty, especially for roses, hydrangeas, and mixed seasonal designs.
