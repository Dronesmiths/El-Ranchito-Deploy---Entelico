How to Keep Flowers Fresh Longer in Pacoima

Once flowers arrive, a little care goes a long way. Pacoima’s dry heat can shorten vase life if flowers are ignored, but basic maintenance can help arrangements stay attractive for days.

  • Keep flowers out of direct sunlight and away from hot windows.
  • Change the water regularly if the bouquet is in a vase.
  • Trim stems at an angle if recommended by the florist.
  • Remove any leaves sitting below the waterline.
  • Place arrangements in a cooler part of the home during peak afternoon heat.

These simple steps matter even more during Valley heat waves. If the bouquet is being displayed at a party or outdoor gathering, bring it inside when not in use so it lasts longer and looks better.
