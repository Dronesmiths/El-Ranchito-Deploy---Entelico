Cornerstone Guide

Seasonal Floral Guide for Every Holiday

Holidays are moments built on tradition, love, and celebration. From the romantic rush of Valentine's Day to the deep gratitude of Mother's Day, flowers are the perfect way to articulate what you feel when words aren't enough. Our florists at Flowers El Ranchito are experts in high-volume holiday demands.

Navigating the Valentine's Day Rush

Valentine's Day is the busiest day of the year in the floral industry. Red roses, particularly the long-stemmed 'Freedom' variety, are the standard of romance. To avoid disappointment, we highly recommend pre-ordering your custom arrangements to guarantee the freshest premium blooms.

Celebrating Mother's Day with Elegance

Mother's Day requires a softer, more elegant touch. Lush, garden-style arrangements featuring peonies, ranunculus, and hydrangeas in pastel pinks and lavenders are our specialty. It's the perfect opportunity to show appreciation with a bespoke design.

Quick Answers (FAQ)

When should I order flowers for Valentine's Day?

To ensure availability and timely delivery, you should place your Valentine's Day order at least one to two weeks in advance. Last-minute orders are subject to limited stock.

What are the most popular Mother's Day flowers?

Peonies, tulips, hydrangeas, and pink roses are the most frequently requested flowers for Mother's Day, symbolizing gratitude, admiration, and unconditional love.

Do you deliver flowers on holidays?

Yes, Flowers El Ranchito provides delivery services across the San Fernando Valley on major floral holidays like Valentine's Day and Mother's Day, though delivery slots fill up quickly.
