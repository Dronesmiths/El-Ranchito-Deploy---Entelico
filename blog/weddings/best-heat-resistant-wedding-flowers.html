Best Heat-Resistant Wedding Flowers

Summer weddings in the San Fernando Valley are beautiful, but the intense heat can be unforgiving on delicate floral arrangements. Choosing heat-resistant wedding flowers is the key to ensuring your bouquets and centerpieces look vibrant in photos taken hours after the ceremony begins.

Top 5 Heat-Resistant Wedding Blooms

Our top recommendations for heat-tolerant blooms include Succulents, Orchids, Calla Lilies, Proteas, and Tropicals like Bird of Paradise. These flowers are naturally adapted to warm climates and retain their moisture and structure even when temperatures soar.

Tips for Keeping Flowers Fresh During the Ceremony

In addition to selecting the right flowers, our expert florists at Flowers El Ranchito use specialized hydration techniques and floral foams to keep stems fed with water throughout your event. We also recommend keeping bouquets in a cool, shaded room until the absolute last minute.

Quick Answers (FAQ)

What are the most heat-resistant flowers for a summer wedding?

The most heat-resistant flowers include Orchids, Succulents, Calla Lilies, Proteas, Zinnias, and tropical flowers like Anthuriums.

Will hydrangeas wilt at an outdoor summer wedding?

Yes, hydrangeas are highly prone to wilting in the heat because they require massive amounts of water. We recommend keeping them strictly for indoor, air-conditioned receptions.

How do I keep my bridal bouquet fresh in the heat?

Keep the stems submerged in fresh water whenever you aren't holding the bouquet, store it in an air-conditioned room or floral cooler, and avoid direct sunlight before photos.
