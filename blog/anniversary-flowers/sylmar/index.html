How to Make Your Anniversary Flowers Last Longer

Once the flowers arrive, proper care helps preserve the beauty of the gift. This is especially important in the San Fernando Valley, where indoor temperatures and afternoon heat can affect longevity. A few simple steps can keep the arrangement fresh and vibrant for days longer.

Flower care tips for recipients

  1. Place flowers in clean water as soon as possible.
  2. Trim stems at an angle before arranging them in a vase.
  3. Keep the bouquet away from direct sunlight and heat vents.
  4. Refresh the water every one to two days.
  5. Remove any fading blooms to protect the rest of the arrangement.
