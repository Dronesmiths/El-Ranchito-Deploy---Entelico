How to Make Fresh Flowers Last Longer at Home

Once flowers arrive, proper care can significantly extend their life. Even the best arrangement benefits from a few simple steps.

Practical Flower Care Tips

  • Trim stems at an angle before placing them in water
  • Use a clean vase with fresh, cool water
  • Remove leaves below the waterline
  • Keep arrangements away from direct sunlight and heat sources
  • Refresh the water every couple of days
  • Re-cut stems if blooms start drinking less water

For North Hills homes, this is especially helpful in rooms that get strong afternoon sun or in spaces where indoor temperatures fluctuate. A little maintenance can keep a bouquet looking fresh, full, and elegant for days longer.

Most fresh flowers last about 5 to 10 days, depending on the flower type, room temperature, and care. Some blooms, like orchids and carnations, can last even longer with proper maintenance.
