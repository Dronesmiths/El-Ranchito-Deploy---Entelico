How to Make Fresh Flowers Last Longer at Home

Once flowers arrive, care matters. Even the freshest bouquet can decline quickly if it isn’t placed and maintained properly. In San Fernando, where indoor and outdoor temperatures can vary significantly throughout the day, simple care steps make a big difference.

Simple Flower Care Instructions

  • Trim stems at an angle before placing them in water
  • Use a clean vase with fresh, cool water
  • Remove leaves that sit below the waterline
  • Keep the arrangement away from direct sun, appliances, and vents
  • Change the water regularly and re-trim stems every few days

If you live near sun-exposed streets or in a home that warms up quickly in the afternoon, consider placing the bouquet in a cooler interior room. Avoid windowsills with harsh light and don’t set flowers near ripening fruit, which can release gases that shorten bloom life. Small adjustments like these can extend the beauty of the arrangement significantly.
