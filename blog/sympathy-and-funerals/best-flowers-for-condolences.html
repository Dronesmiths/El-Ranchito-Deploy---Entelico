Best Flowers for Condolences

Choosing the right flowers to express condolences is an important decision. The types of flowers you select convey a specific message of sympathy, peace, and remembrance to the grieving family.

The Symbolic Meaning of White Lilies

White Lilies are universally recognized as the quintessential sympathy flower. They symbolize the restoration of innocence to the soul of the departed and offer a majestic, peaceful presence. White roses and carnations are also highly appropriate, representing pure love and remembrance.

Potted Plants vs. Cut Flowers

While cut flower arrangements are beautiful, many people choose to send potted plants, such as Peace Lilies or Orchids, to the family's home. A living plant serves as a long-lasting tribute that the family can nurture in memory of their loved one.

Quick Answers (FAQ)

What do white lilies symbolize at a funeral?

White lilies symbolize purity, peace, and the return of the soul to a state of innocence. They are the most traditional funeral flower.

Are red roses appropriate for a funeral?

Yes, red roses are appropriate, especially if sent by close family members or a spouse, as they symbolize deep, enduring love and respect.

What is a Peace Lily?

A Peace Lily is an elegant, low-maintenance potted plant featuring dark green leaves and white blossoms. It is a very popular sympathy gift sent to a family's home.
