How to Make Your Rose Bouquet Last Longer

Once your bouquet arrives, a few simple steps can extend its beauty. This is especially helpful in Pacoima, where indoor temperatures can vary and dry air may reduce hydration faster than expected.

  • Trim stems at an angle before placing roses in water.
  • Use a clean vase with fresh, cool water.
  • Keep flowers away from direct sunlight and heat vents.
  • Change the water regularly.
  • Remove any leaves sitting below the waterline.

These care habits help preserve the bouquet’s look and give the recipient more time to enjoy the flowers at home or at work.
