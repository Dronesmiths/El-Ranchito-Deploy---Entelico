Privacy Policy

At Flowers El Ranchito, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, when we may share it, and the choices you have regarding your information when you visit our website, place an order, subscribe to updates, or contact our team. Because floral purchases often involve meaningful life moments, we want this policy to be clear, trustworthy, and easy to understand.

Information We Collect

We collect information in a few different ways so we can process orders, improve our website, and communicate with you effectively. Some information is provided directly by you, and some is collected automatically when you browse our site.

Information You Provide to Us

When you place an order, request a custom arrangement, join our mailing list, submit a contact form, or call us for assistance, you may provide personal details such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing and delivery address
  • Recipient information for flower deliveries
  • Message card text and special delivery instructions
  • Event details for weddings, sympathy services, birthdays, anniversaries, or corporate floral needs

Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, we may automatically collect technical information when you visit our site. This can include your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, referring website, approximate location, and time spent on the site. This data helps us understand how visitors use our website and allows us to improve performance, navigation, and content.

Information From Third Parties

In some cases, we may receive information from third-party services that support our operations, such as payment processors, analytics tools, delivery partners, and marketing platforms. We only use this information as needed to provide services, maintain security, and improve customer experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for legitimate business purposes related to floral ordering, customer service, website functionality, and communication. The primary reason we collect personal data is to fulfill your requests accurately and efficiently.

Order Fulfillment and Delivery

Your name, address, phone number, and delivery instructions help us prepare and deliver your floral arrangement. This is especially important when timing matters, such as same-day deliveries, memorial services, or event installations.

Customer Communication

We may use your contact details to confirm orders, provide delivery updates, answer questions, or resolve issues related to an order. If a substitution is needed due to seasonal flower availability, we may contact you to discuss options.

Marketing and Promotions

If you opt in, we may send you promotional emails, seasonal offers, holiday reminders, or product announcements. You can opt out at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.

Website Improvement and Analytics

We may analyze website usage to understand which pages are most helpful, how visitors interact with our content, and where improvements are needed. This helps us make the site easier to use and more relevant to our customers.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when necessary to operate our business, complete your order, comply with the law, or protect our rights.

Recipient Why We Share
Payment processors To process transactions securely
Delivery and logistics partners To complete floral deliveries accurately
Email and marketing platforms To send newsletters or promotions if you have opted in
Analytics providers To understand website performance and visitor behavior
Legal authorities When required by law, court order, or governmental request

Service Providers and Vendors

Some third-party providers help us run essential parts of our business, such as payment processing, website hosting, email delivery, and customer support tools. These providers are expected to handle data responsibly and only use it for authorized purposes.

Business Transfers

If Flowers El Ranchito is involved in a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable legal requirements.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to improve functionality and measure performance. These tools help remember preferences, keep items in your cart, and understand how visitors interact with our pages.

Types of Cookies We May Use

  1. Essential cookies: Required for basic site operation, including secure checkout and page navigation.
  2. Performance cookies: Help us measure traffic and identify areas where the site can be improved.
  3. Functional cookies: Remember user preferences and settings.
  4. Marketing cookies: May be used to deliver relevant advertisements or promotions if enabled.

Your Cookie Choices

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. If you disable certain cookies, some features of the site may not function properly. For more information on managing cookies, consult your browser’s help resources.

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to help protect your personal information. While no website or online system can guarantee complete security, we work to reduce risks and maintain a safe environment for customer data.

Protecting your information is part of protecting your experience. Whether you are ordering sympathy flowers, anniversary roses, or a custom bouquet, we aim to handle your details with care and discretion.

Security Measures May Include

  • Secure payment processing
  • Restricted access to customer data
  • Regular system maintenance and updates
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity
  • Operational procedures designed to reduce unauthorized access

Your Privacy Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights related to your personal information. These rights can include the ability to access, correct, delete, or limit certain uses of your data. You may also have the right to opt out of marketing communications.

Access and Correction

If you would like to review or update information you previously provided, you may contact us and request assistance. We will make reasonable efforts to help keep your information accurate and current.

Deletion Requests

In certain circumstances, you may ask us to delete personal information we maintain about you. Some information may need to be retained for legal, accounting, security, or order-processing purposes.

Marketing Opt-Out

If you no longer wish to receive promotional emails, you can unsubscribe using the link in the message or contact us directly. Please note that even if you opt out of marketing, we may still send order-related or service-related messages.

Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that a child has submitted personal data to us without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, such as social media pages, payment portals, or partner resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party service you visit.

How Long We Keep Information

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including processing orders, maintaining business records, resolving disputes, and meeting legal obligations. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information and the reason it was collected.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or business operations. When we make updates, we may revise the “last updated” date on our website. We encourage you to review this page periodically so you stay informed about how we protect your information.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, your personal information, or how we handle data, please contact us using the information provided on our website. We are happy to help explain our practices and address concerns related to your privacy.

Why Privacy Matters for Flower Orders

Flower purchases often involve personal moments, private sentiments, and sensitive delivery details. Whether you are sending a bouquet for a celebration, a remembrance service, or a surprise delivery, we understand that discretion matters. Our privacy practices are designed to support that trust every step of the way.
