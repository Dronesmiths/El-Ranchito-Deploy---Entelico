Rose Bouquet Care After Delivery

Once your bouquet arrives, a few simple steps can help your roses stay beautiful longer. Fresh flowers last best when they are placed in clean water, kept away from direct sunlight, and protected from heat sources. In Sylmar homes where afternoon temperatures can build quickly, this is especially important.

Trim stems at an angle, remove any leaves below the waterline, and change the water regularly. If the bouquet includes a vase arrangement, keep it in a cool indoor location rather than near windows with strong sun exposure. Good care helps the roses remain vibrant and fragrant for days.
