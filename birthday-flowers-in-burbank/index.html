How to Choose the Right Birthday Bouquet

A great birthday arrangement should match both the occasion and the person. If you’re unsure what to send, think through these simple factors.

  1. The recipient’s personality: Bold and energetic, soft and romantic, modern and minimalist, or traditional and classic.
  2. The delivery setting: Home, office, restaurant, studio, hospital, or event venue.
