How to Order the Perfect Custom Bouquet

Ordering a custom bouquet should be simple, but the best results come when you share a few key details. The more specific you are, the more accurately the design can reflect your vision. If you’re not sure what to choose, Flowers El Ranchito can help guide the process.

  1. Choose the occasion. Birthday, sympathy, anniversary, corporate gift, thank-you, get-well, or “just because.”
  2. Share the style. Romantic, modern, rustic, elegant, colorful, soft, or dramatic.
  3. Select the color palette. You can request bold jewel tones, pastel shades, neutral whites, or a mix.
  4. Note any flower preferences. Some customers love roses, orchids, lilies, sunflowers, or tulips.
  5. Provide delivery details. Glendale neighborhoods, business addresses, apartment buildings, and event venues all help with timing and presentation.
