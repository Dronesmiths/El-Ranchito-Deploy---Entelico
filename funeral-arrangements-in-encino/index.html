Choosing the Right Flowers for a Memorial Service

Flower selection is often guided by tradition, symbolism, and the preferences of the family. Some blooms are chosen for their timeless elegance, while others are selected because they carried special meaning to the person being remembered.

Common flowers and their meanings

  • White lilies: purity, peace, and the restored innocence of the soul
  • Roses: love, honor, and devotion, with color choices shaping the tone
  • Chrysanthemums: remembrance and respect, especially in formal memorial settings
  • Carnations: admiration, affection, and enduring memory
  • Orchids: grace, beauty, and lasting tribute
  • Gladiolus: strength of character and moral integrity

In Encino, many families prefer white and green palettes because they feel timeless and serene. Others choose soft pinks, lavender, or deep reds to create a more personalized expression. If the service is in a sunlit venue near Lake Balboa or in a home with open-air gathering space, we may recommend blooms that travel well in warm weather and hold their shape throughout the day.
