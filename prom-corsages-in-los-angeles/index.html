How to Keep a Prom Corsage Looking Fresh All Night

Once the corsage leaves the shop, a few simple steps can help preserve its appearance. Keep it in a cool place until it is time to leave. Avoid leaving it in a hot car, especially during warmer inland afternoons. If the event includes a long pre-prom photo stop, store the corsage away from direct sun until it is time to put it on.

For wrist corsages, placement should be snug enough to stay secure but not so tight that it becomes uncomfortable during dinner or dancing. For pin-on styles, careful placement on the left lapel or dress strap area can help the arrangement sit flat and photograph well. If you are heading from a home in the Valley to a venue near the coast, or making multiple stops across the city, keeping the flowers protected during transit is especially important.
