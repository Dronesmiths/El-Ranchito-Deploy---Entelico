Keeping Tropical Flowers Looking Their Best

To get the most out of your tropical arrangement, a few simple care tips go a long way. Keep the flowers away from direct heat vents, intense afternoon sun, and fruit bowls. Refresh the water as recommended, and trim stems if instructed. In Tarzana’s warm climate, it’s also smart to place arrangements in cooler indoor areas when possible.

These flowers are naturally durable, but a little care helps preserve their color and form even longer. That makes them a dependable option for busy households, offices, and event hosts who want beauty without constant maintenance.
