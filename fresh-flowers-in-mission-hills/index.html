Care Tips to Make Fresh Flowers Last Longer

Once the flowers arrive, a few simple steps can extend their beauty:

  1. Trim stems at an angle before placing them in water.
  2. Use a clean vase and fresh, cool water.
  3. Remove leaves that would sit below the waterline.
  4. Keep the arrangement away from direct sun, heating vents, and ripening fruit.
  5. Refresh the water every one to two days.

These small habits can make a noticeable difference, especially in Mission Hills homes where indoor temperatures may fluctuate throughout the day. Proper care helps roses open evenly, keeps greenery crisp, and allows mixed arrangements to maintain their shape.
