Planning Tips for a Smooth Wedding Flower Experience

  1. Start early so your florist can understand your venue, color palette, and budget goals.
  2. Bring inspiration photos, but stay open to seasonal substitutions that improve freshness and value.
  3. Share your timeline, including ceremony start time, photo sessions, and reception transitions.
  4. Tell your florist whether your wedding is indoors, outdoors, or both.
  5. Discuss how many bouquets, boutonnieres, centerpieces, and ceremony pieces you need.

Good planning helps avoid unnecessary stress. It also gives your florist time to source the best flowers, coordinate delivery, and ensure that each arrangement is designed to suit the exact conditions of your Granada Hills wedding.
