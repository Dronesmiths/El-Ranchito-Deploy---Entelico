Seasonal Flower Planning for Glendale Weddings

Seasonality plays a major role in wedding flower selection. Glendale enjoys a warm Mediterranean climate, which means spring and fall are especially popular wedding seasons. Summer weddings can be stunning, but they require more planning for hydration, transport, and bloom selection. Winter weddings often benefit from deeper tones and more structured flowers that hold up beautifully in cooler evenings.

Spring Weddings

Spring in Glendale brings fresh color and some of the best blooms of the year. Couples often choose peonies, ranunculus, freesia, and delicate garden roses. These flowers pair beautifully with light linens, outdoor ceremonies, and the bright natural light common in the foothill neighborhoods.

Summer Weddings

For summer events, we recommend durable flowers with excellent heat tolerance. Roses, orchids, carnations, lisianthus, and selected greenery can perform well when handled properly. We also design with shade exposure and timing in mind, especially for afternoon ceremonies in sun-drenched locations near Kenneth Village or hillside homes.

Fall Weddings

Fall weddings in Glendale are ideal for rich palettes and layered textures. Think burgundy roses, rust-toned blooms, deep greenery, and elegant neutrals. These arrangements work beautifully with golden-hour photography and warm interior lighting.

Winter Weddings

Winter florals can feel sophisticated and timeless. White roses, amaryllis, anemones, orchids, and evergreen accents create a refined look that suits formal indoor venues and evening receptions.
