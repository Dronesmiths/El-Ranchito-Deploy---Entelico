Care Tips for Tropical Flower Arrangements

With the right care, tropical arrangements can stay beautiful for an extended time. While each design is unique, a few general practices help protect freshness and appearance.

Keep them out of direct heat

Place arrangements away from full afternoon sun, vents, and appliances. In Valley Village homes, that often means avoiding windows that face intense western exposure during the hottest part of the day.

Refresh water carefully

Clean water helps maintain stem health. If your arrangement is in a vase, change the water regularly and follow the care guidance included with the delivery.

Trim and reposition as needed

Some tropical stems benefit from a light trim after a few days. Repositioning leaves or blooms can also help the arrangement maintain its shape and visual balance.

Use the right placement

Choose a stable surface where the arrangement can be appreciated without risk of tipping. Tropical designs often have height and architectural structure, so placement matters as much as the flowers themselves.
