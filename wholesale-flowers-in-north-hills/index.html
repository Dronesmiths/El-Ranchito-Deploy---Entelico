Wholesale flowers can last longer when handled properly because they are typically purchased with professional use in mind and can be processed quickly after delivery. Proper hydration, cool storage, and prompt arrangement are essential.

Yes. Small retailers, cafes, salons, and boutique offices in North Hills often benefit from wholesale buying because it provides better value and more control over presentation.
