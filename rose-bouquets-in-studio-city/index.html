Care Tips for Rose Bouquets

If you want your roses to last as long as possible, a few simple steps make a big difference. These tips are especially useful in Studio City’s warm afternoons and dry indoor air from air conditioning.

  1. Trim the stems at an angle before placing them in water.
  2. Use a clean vase with fresh room-temperature water.
  3. Remove any leaves that would sit below the waterline.
  4. Keep the bouquet away from direct sun, vents, and ripening fruit.
  5. Refresh the water and recut stems every couple of days.

These small habits help preserve the bouquet’s shape, color, and fragrance. They also make a noticeable difference in how long the roses remain photo-ready on a dining table, entryway console, or office reception counter.
