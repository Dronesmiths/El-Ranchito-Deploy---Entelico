Prom Corsage Care Tips for Event Day

Even the best corsage benefits from a little care. Keep it in a cool place until it is time to wear it, avoid direct sunlight for long periods, and handle it gently when putting it on. If you are traveling across Granada Hills or making multiple stops before the event, store it carefully in the car away from heat and direct air vents.

For wrist corsages, make sure the band fits comfortably without pinching. For pin-on styles, check placement early so the flowers sit flat and secure. If you are unsure, our team can advise you on the best wearing method when you pick up your flowers.
