Care Tips to Help Roses Last Longer

Even the best bouquet benefits from proper care after delivery. If you want roses to look fresh for as long as possible, a few simple steps can make a real difference.

  1. Trim the stems at an angle before placing them in water.
  2. Use a clean vase with fresh, room-temperature water.
  3. Remove any leaves that sit below the waterline.
  4. Keep the bouquet away from direct sun, vents, and hot windows.
  5. Refresh the water regularly and re-trim the stems if needed.

These tips are especially helpful in Toluca Lake, where warm afternoons and indoor climate shifts can dry flowers out faster than expected. A little care goes a long way in preserving the bouquet’s beauty.
