How to Choose the Right Sympathy Arrangement

If you are unsure what to send, start with the setting and your relationship to the family. A close relative may choose a larger tribute, while a coworker, neighbor, or friend may prefer a modest arrangement that expresses care without feeling excessive. The tone of the relationship should guide the style of the flowers.

Helpful selection tips

  1. Choose a palette that feels calm. White, cream, pale pink, lavender, and soft green are common sympathy choices.
  2. Match the venue. Home deliveries often suit baskets and bouquets, while services may call for standing sprays or wreaths.
  3. Consider the person being honored. Favorite colors or meaningful blooms can personalize the tribute.
  4. Keep the message simple. A short condolence card is often the most respectful choice.
  5. Allow for local conditions. In Sylmar’s warm climate, durable flowers can help the arrangement last longer.

Popular sympathy flowers include lilies, roses, carnations, orchids, chrysanthemums, hydrangea, and seasonal greenery. Lilies often symbolize peace and remembrance, while roses convey love and honor. Carnations provide structure and long-lasting beauty, and orchids can create a refined, understated tribute. We can help you choose blooms that align with the sentiment you want to express.
