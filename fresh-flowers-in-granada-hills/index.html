What Makes Fresh Flowers Truly Fresh?

Freshness is not a marketing phrase—it is a standard built on sourcing, handling, timing, and delivery. A beautiful arrangement starts long before it reaches your doorstep. It begins with flowers selected for color, form, and vase life, then properly conditioned so they can absorb water efficiently and maintain structure.

Key freshness factors we prioritize

  • Careful stem selection: We choose blooms with strong petals, healthy leaves, and balanced opening stages for a fuller presentation.
  • Proper hydration: Flowers are conditioned in clean water and prepared for transport to reduce stress.
  • Temperature awareness: We protect arrangements from unnecessary heat exposure during preparation and delivery.
  • Timing: The quicker flowers move from preparation to recipient, the better they look and last.

In a place like Granada Hills, where the climate can shift from cool early mornings to intense afternoon warmth, freshness is especially important. A florist who understands this will design and deliver with care instead of treating flowers like a generic product.
