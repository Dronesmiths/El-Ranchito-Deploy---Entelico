How to Make a Thoughtful Selection

Consider the relationship

Your relationship to the deceased often helps determine the right type of floral tribute. Immediate family members may choose larger, more prominent pieces such as casket sprays or standing sprays. Friends, coworkers, neighbors, and extended family members often select wreaths, bouquets, or vase arrangements that express sympathy respectfully.

Match the tone of the service

Some services are deeply traditional, while others are more celebratory and reflective. Woodland Hills families may host a formal religious service, a modest graveside gathering, or a contemporary celebration of life in a banquet hall or private home. The arrangement should fit the tone. White and green designs often feel serene and classic, while softer mixed palettes can bring warmth and personality.

Think about placement

Where the flowers will be displayed matters. A standing spray looks powerful near an altar or entrance. A compact vase arrangement may be ideal for a reception table or family home. If the service is in a smaller chapel or a room with limited space, oversized pieces may feel overwhelming. We help you choose a scale that complements the venue rather than crowding it.

Popular Funeral Flowers and Their Meanings

Lilies

Lilies are among the most common funeral flowers because they symbolize restored innocence, peace, and the soul’s return to serenity. Their elegant shape makes them ideal for formal tributes.

Roses

Roses communicate love, respect, and remembrance. White roses are often used for purity and reverence, while red roses may express deep love and devotion. Pink and yellow roses can add warmth and gratitude to a tribute.

Carnations

Carnations are long-lasting and versatile, which makes them a practical choice for services that extend over multiple days. They are often used in sprays, wreaths, and mixed sympathy arrangements.

Orchids

Orchids bring elegance and quiet sophistication. They are especially fitting for refined memorials or custom designs intended to feel serene and graceful.
