  1. Standing Sprays: A traditional tribute often displayed on an easel at a funeral or memorial service.
  2. Sympathy Wreaths: Circular arrangements symbolizing remembrance and eternal love.
  3. Casket Sprays: Larger arrangements designed for the casket at a service.
  4. Basket Arrangements: Versatile and suitable for delivery to a home, office, or service venue.
