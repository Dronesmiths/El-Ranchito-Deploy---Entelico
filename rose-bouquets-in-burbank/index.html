How to Keep Rose Bouquets Fresh Longer

Freshness is one of the most important parts of a great rose bouquet. Once the bouquet arrives, simple care steps can extend its life and preserve the beauty of the blooms.

Rose Care Tips After Delivery

  • Trim stems at an angle before placing roses in water.
  • Use a clean vase with fresh, cool water.
  • Remove leaves that would sit below the waterline.
  • Keep the bouquet away from direct sunlight and heat vents.
  • Refresh water regularly for better bloom longevity.

These steps are especially important in Burbank, where indoor temperature swings can happen quickly. A bouquet left near a bright window in a warm apartment off Glenoaks Boulevard will age faster than one placed in a cooler interior room. Small care habits make a big difference.
