Flower Care Tips for Warm Granada Hills Weather

Because Granada Hills can run warm, especially during spring afternoons, it helps to know how to keep Mother’s Day flowers looking fresh longer. Even the most beautiful arrangement benefits from a little care once it arrives.

Simple care steps

  1. Place flowers away from direct sunlight and hot windows.
  2. Keep the vase filled with clean water as instructed.
  3. Trim stems if recommended to help with water absorption.
  4. Remove any wilted petals or leaves to maintain appearance.
  5. Keep arrangements away from appliances, vents, and heat sources.

These small steps can help preserve the bouquet well beyond the first day, so Mom can enjoy the flowers through the weekend and beyond.
