How to Keep Fresh Flowers Looking Their Best

Even the most beautiful bouquet needs proper care once it arrives. If you want your flowers to last, a few simple habits can make a major difference—especially in Sylmar’s warm, dry climate.

Best care practices

  • Trim stems at an angle before placing them in water.
  • Use a clean vase to reduce bacteria buildup.
  • Change the water regularly.
  • Keep flowers out of direct sunlight and away from heat vents.
  • Remove wilted petals or leaves promptly.
  • Follow any care instructions included with the arrangement.

If the flowers are going to a business near Foothill Boulevard or a home exposed to afternoon sun, placement is especially important. A bouquet near a window with strong light may fade faster than one kept in a cooler interior space. Small adjustments can add days to the life of your arrangement.
