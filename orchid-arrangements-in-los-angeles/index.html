How to Care for Orchid Arrangements

One of the biggest reasons customers love orchids is their longevity. With basic care, they can remain beautiful far longer than many other cut flowers. In Los Angeles, where indoor climates can vary from coastal humidity near Santa Monica to dry heat in the Valley, proper placement is especially important.

Simple orchid care tips

  • Keep orchids in bright, indirect light rather than direct sun.
  • Avoid placing them near vents, heaters, or drafty windows.
  • Water only as recommended for the specific arrangement or plant type.
  • Use a stable indoor environment with moderate temperatures.
  • Rotate the arrangement occasionally for even light exposure.

If your orchid arrangement is being displayed in a home near the coast, be mindful of salt air and breezes from open windows. If it’s in the Valley, avoid hot sun exposure through large glass doors or windows. These small adjustments help preserve the arrangement’s beauty.
