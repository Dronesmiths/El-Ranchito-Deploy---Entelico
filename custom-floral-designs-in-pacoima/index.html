How to Care for Custom Floral Arrangements

Once your arrangement arrives, a few simple steps can help it last longer and continue looking its best.

Keep Flowers Cool and Out of Direct Sunlight

In a sunny Pacoima home, place arrangements away from windows, vents, and kitchens when possible. Heat speeds up dehydration, especially during valley warm spells.

Refresh Water Regularly

If your arrangement includes a vase, change the water as directed and trim stems slightly to improve hydration. Clean water helps preserve freshness and reduce bacterial buildup.

Follow Placement Guidance for Special Pieces

Large event pieces or sympathy arrangements may have specific placement needs. Follow any care instructions so the flowers remain secure and visually balanced throughout the event or display period.
