Seasonal Wedding Flowers That Work Beautifully in Burbank

Choosing flowers by season helps you achieve the best quality, color, and value. It also allows us to recommend blooms that naturally perform well in the region’s climate.

Spring

Spring weddings are ideal for peonies, ranunculus, tulips, sweet peas, hellebores, and garden roses. This season is perfect for soft, layered arrangements with a fresh and airy feel.

Summer

Summer weddings in Burbank require blooms that can manage heat and bright light. Roses, orchids, alstroemeria, lisianthus, carnations in modern styling, and hardy greenery can all create stunning results while holding up better through the day.

Fall

Autumn wedding palettes often include deep burgundy, rust, peach, amber, and cream tones. Dahlias, roses, chrysanthemums, orchids, and textured foliage pair well with the warm valley atmosphere and golden evening light.

Winter

Winter weddings can feel crisp and elegant with white roses, anemones, amaryllis, evergreen accents, and rich greenery. In Burbank, winter florals can be especially dramatic when paired with candlelight and metallic accents.

Wedding Flower Tips for Burbank Venues and Conditions

If you are planning a wedding in Burbank, a few local details can help your flowers look their best from arrival to last call.

  • Schedule delivery with traffic in mind: Freeway congestion near the 5 and 134 can affect timing, especially during peak hours.

  • Think about photos: Your flowers should look beautiful from close-up detail shots and wide venue images alike.
