What flowers are best for sympathy?

White lilies, roses, orchids, hydrangea, carnations, and mixed greenery are among the most common sympathy choices because they feel peaceful, elegant, and respectful.

Can you deliver sympathy flowers the same day in Glendale?

Yes, same-day delivery is often available depending on the order time and flower availability. We recommend placing your order as early as possible for the best results.

Should I send flowers to the home or the service?

Either can be appropriate. Home delivery is often best for a more personal gesture, while service delivery is ideal for formal tributes displayed during the ceremony.

What message should I include with sympathy flowers?

Short, sincere messages work best. Simple phrases such as “With deepest sympathy,” “Thinking of you,” or “With heartfelt condolences” are always appropriate.
