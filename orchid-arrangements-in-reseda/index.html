How to Care for Orchid Arrangements After Delivery

One of the biggest advantages of orchid arrangements is that they can stay beautiful for an extended period when cared for correctly. Simple care habits can make a major difference in how long the blooms last and how fresh they appear.

Keep them in stable indoor conditions

Place orchids away from direct sun, heating vents, and sudden drafts. In Reseda homes and offices, this often means choosing a spot with bright indirect light rather than a windowsill exposed to afternoon heat.

Water carefully, not excessively

Orchids do best when they are not overwatered. Depending on the arrangement style, the care routine may vary, but the general rule is to keep the roots and medium lightly moist without letting them sit in standing water.

Rotate and refresh display areas

If the arrangement is placed in a business lobby or high-traffic home area, occasional rotation can help the blooms receive even light. Keeping the surrounding area clean and dust-free also helps orchids look their best.
