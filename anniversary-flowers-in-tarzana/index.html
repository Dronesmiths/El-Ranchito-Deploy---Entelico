How to Choose the Right Anniversary Flowers

If you want your gift to feel meaningful, a little planning goes a long way. The best anniversary flowers reflect both the occasion and the recipient’s style.

1. Consider the milestone

A first anniversary may call for something bright and youthful, while a 25th or 50th anniversary often pairs well with a more refined and luxurious arrangement.

2. Think about their favorite colors

Flowers feel more personal when they match the recipient’s taste. Soft neutrals, rich reds, garden pastels, or vivid jewel tones can all work beautifully depending on the person receiving them.

3. Match the setting

If the flowers are going to a Tarzana home, a larger arrangement may work well as a centerpiece. For office delivery, a compact but elegant design may be more practical. For dinner delivery, a bouquet that’s easy to carry and display can be ideal.

4. Decide on the message

Do you want the arrangement to feel romantic, celebratory, luxurious, or sentimental? Once you know the tone, it becomes much easier to choose the right style.
