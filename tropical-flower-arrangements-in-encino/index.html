How to Choose the Right Tropical Arrangement

Choosing the right arrangement depends on purpose, size, and style. A compact orchid design might be ideal for a small apartment or office desk, while a tall bird-of-paradise arrangement may be better for a foyer or event entrance. Consider the following before ordering:

  1. Location: Will the flowers sit in direct sunlight, a shaded room, or a climate-controlled office?
  2. Scale: Do you need something subtle, medium-sized, or a high-impact centerpiece?
  3. Style: Are you aiming for modern minimalism, lush luxury, or colorful abundance?
  4. Occasion: Is this a personal gift, business display, or event installation?
  5. Longevity: Do you want a design known for structure and staying power?

If you are unsure, we can help you choose a design that fits both the space and the sentiment behind the flowers. That guidance is especially useful for customers sending flowers to offices, homes, or event venues throughout Encino and the greater San Fernando Valley.

Care Tips for Tropical Flower Arrangements

Tropical flowers are admired for their dramatic beauty, but they also benefit from thoughtful care. While each arrangement is unique, a few general practices can help extend its visual life:

Keep the arrangement away from direct heat, strong afternoon sun, and vents that blow warm or dry air. Refresh the water as needed, trim stems if instructed, and display the flowers in a stable indoor setting where temperatures remain moderate.

Encino’s warm climate makes indoor placement especially important. A bouquet placed near a sunny window in a house off Ventura Boulevard or in a room that traps heat in the afternoon may not last as long as one kept in a cooler, shaded area. For businesses in busy storefronts or office suites, a climate-controlled environment is usually the best choice.
