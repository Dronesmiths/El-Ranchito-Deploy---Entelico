How to Care for Orchid Arrangements

One of the biggest advantages of orchids is that they can last significantly longer than many traditional cut flowers. Still, proper care makes a difference. Flowers El Ranchito provides arrangements designed for longevity, but a few simple practices can help preserve their beauty.

Helpful orchid care tips

  1. Keep the arrangement away from direct afternoon sun, especially near west-facing windows.
  2. Avoid placing orchids near heating vents, fireplaces, or strong air conditioning currents.
  3. Use clean water and follow any care instructions provided with the arrangement.
  4. In dry Woodland Hills air, monitor moisture levels more carefully than you would in a humid climate.
  5. Remove wilted blooms gently to maintain the arrangement’s clean appearance.

Because the local climate can be dry and warm, orchids do best when they are displayed in a stable indoor environment. That is one reason they are so popular in Woodland Hills homes and offices—they thrive when cared for thoughtfully and look luxurious throughout their lifespan.
