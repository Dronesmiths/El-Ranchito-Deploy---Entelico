How to Care for Orchid Arrangements

Orchids are known for their elegance, but they are also relatively easy to maintain when you follow a few simple care steps. Proper care can help extend the life of your arrangement and keep it looking fresh far longer than many other floral options.

  1. Place orchids in bright, indirect light. Avoid harsh direct sun, especially during warm Studio City afternoons.
  2. Keep them away from vents and heat sources. Dry air from HVAC systems can shorten bloom life.
  3. Water carefully. Overwatering is the most common mistake. Follow the care guidance provided with your arrangement.
  4. Rotate the arrangement occasionally. This helps the blooms age evenly and maintain a balanced appearance.
  5. Remove spent blooms when needed. This keeps the arrangement looking neat and well maintained.
