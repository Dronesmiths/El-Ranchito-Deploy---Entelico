Care Tips to Help Tropical Arrangements Last Longer

One reason tropical arrangements are so popular is that many of them are naturally long-lasting. With a few simple care practices, you can extend their beauty even further.

  1. Keep them indoors: Tropical flowers prefer stable temperatures and should be kept away from direct sun, vents, and drafts.
  2. Refresh water as directed: Clean water supports longer vase life and better bloom appearance.
  3. Trim stems if needed: Fresh cuts can help flowers absorb water more effectively.
  4. Avoid fruit bowls: Ripening fruit releases ethylene gas, which can shorten bloom life.
  5. Display with care: Place arrangements where they can be admired without being crowded or bumped.

These simple steps are especially helpful in Burbank homes and businesses where indoor air systems, frequent door openings, and direct afternoon sun can affect floral longevity.
