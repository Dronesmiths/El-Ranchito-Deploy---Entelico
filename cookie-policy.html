1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer, tablet, or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used across the internet to make websites work more efficiently, remember certain actions or preferences, and help site owners understand how visitors use their pages.

At Flowers El Ranchito, cookies may help the website remember items in your cart, keep you signed in if applicable, understand which pages are most useful, and support smoother navigation from browsing to checkout. Cookies do not usually identify you by name on their own, but they can be associated with device data, browser behavior, or other online identifiers.

How cookies support your browsing experience

  • They help core pages load and function correctly.
  • They remember preferences such as language or region settings.
  • They allow us to analyze traffic patterns and improve usability.
  • They may help personalize promotions or content based on browsing behavior.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

Different cookies serve different purposes. Some are necessary for the site to operate, while others help us understand performance or support marketing efforts. Below is a clear overview of the main categories of cookies you may encounter on our website.

Cookie Type Purpose Typical Outcome
Strictly Necessary Enable essential site functions such as navigation, security, and checkout The website works properly
Performance / Analytics Measure how visitors use the site and identify improvements Better usability and content quality
Functional Remember preferences and enhance convenience A more personalized experience
Advertising / Marketing Help deliver relevant ads or measure campaign performance More relevant promotional messaging

Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are essential to the functioning of the site. Without them, certain features may not work correctly. For example, they may support secure page navigation, shopping cart functionality, and form submissions. Because these cookies are required for basic operation, they are typically enabled by default.

Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site. They may show us which pages are visited most often, how long users stay on a page, and where visitors may encounter friction. This helps Flowers El Ranchito improve product pages, streamline checkout, and make the site easier to use for customers searching for arrangements, delivery details, or event flowers.

Functional cookies

Functional cookies allow the site to remember choices you make, such as your preferred location, previously viewed products, or user interface settings. These cookies are designed to make repeat visits more convenient and reduce the need to re-enter information.

Advertising cookies

Advertising or marketing cookies may be used to deliver more relevant offers or measure the effectiveness of campaigns. For example, if you viewed a seasonal bouquet collection, cookies may help us understand that interest and tailor future messaging accordingly. These cookies are generally managed through third-party platforms subject to their own policies.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have choices when it comes to cookies. Most web browsers allow you to control or delete cookies through your browser settings. You may be able to block certain types of cookies, clear stored cookies, or set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being placed on your device.

Please note that disabling some cookies may affect the functionality of the website. For example, essential features like cart persistence, secure forms, or preference memory may not work as intended if certain cookies are turned off.

Ways to manage cookies

  • Adjust browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • Use device-level privacy controls where available.
  • Review third-party platform opt-out tools if applicable.
  • Clear cookies regularly if you prefer a more limited browsing history.
