Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to understand how you interact with our website, to remember your preferences, and to improve your browsing experience. This includes essential cookies required for site functionality and analytical cookies to help us improve our services.

3. Managing Cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, or you can use our cookie consent banner to manage your preferences. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.
