Common types of funeral arrangements

  1. Standing sprays: Large easel-mounted floral tributes placed near the casket or memorial display.
  2. Casket sprays: Full or half-casket arrangements chosen by immediate family.
  3. Sympathy bouquets: Smaller arrangements sent to the home or service venue.
  4. Wreaths and crosses: Traditional shapes often used for religious services.
  5. Urn and memorial arrangements: Floral designs created for cremation services and remembrance tables.
