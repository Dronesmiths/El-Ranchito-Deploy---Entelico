Types of Funeral Arrangements We Commonly Provide

Every family’s needs are different, and every tribute should reflect the person being remembered. That is why funeral arrangements can take many forms, from classic sympathy flowers to more personalized tributes that highlight favorite colors, flowers, or themes.

Casket Sprays

Casket sprays are among the most visible floral tributes at a service. These arrangements are typically placed atop the casket and often serve as the centerpiece of the floral display. We create full and half casket sprays in styles ranging from traditional whites and greens to richer color palettes that reflect a loved one’s personality.

Standing Sprays and Easel Arrangements

Standing sprays are ideal for funeral homes, chapels, and memorial services where flowers need to be visible from a distance. They are often sent by family, friends, colleagues, or community groups. A standing spray can be formal and classic or softer and more garden-inspired, depending on the tone of the service.

Wreaths and Hearts

Wreaths and heart-shaped tributes are especially meaningful for families who want a symbolic expression of eternal remembrance or enduring love. These designs are often chosen for memorial services in Sherman Oaks neighborhoods where family traditions are deeply valued and personal details matter.

Sympathy Bouquets for Homes and Family Gatherings

Not every tribute belongs at a formal service. Many families prefer sympathy bouquets delivered to a home, where they can provide comfort in the days surrounding the memorial. These arrangements are usually smaller, easier to place, and designed to bring calm to a living room, entryway, or dining table.

In times of grief, the most meaningful floral tributes are often the ones that feel quietly sincere, locally reliable, and beautifully composed.
