What Makes a Flower Arrangement Last Longer in North Hills?

Longevity depends on both design and care. Because North Hills can experience hot, dry weather, flowers need proper handling before and after delivery. We condition stems carefully, trim them for water uptake, and design arrangements with freshness in mind. Once the flowers are in your home or office, a few simple steps can help extend their life.

  1. Keep the arrangement out of direct sunlight whenever possible.
  2. Place it away from heat sources, vents, and appliances.
  3. Refresh the water regularly if the arrangement is in a vase.
  4. Trim stems if recommended for the specific design.
  5. Remove any fading blooms to help the remaining flowers stay attractive.

These practices matter even more in the Valley’s warmer months, when indoor air can be dry and outdoor temperatures can rise quickly. A well-designed arrangement should be able to handle the local environment while still looking refined.
