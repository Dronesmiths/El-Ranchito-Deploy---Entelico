Orchid Care Tips for Longer Enjoyment

Orchids are admired not only for their appearance, but for how long they can remain attractive with proper care. If you receive an orchid arrangement from Flowers El Ranchito, a few simple habits can help extend its beauty.

Keep It Out of Harsh Direct Sun

Bright light is good, but direct, intense afternoon sun can be too much, especially in Sherman Oaks where rooms can heat up quickly. A spot with filtered light is often best.

Avoid Overwatering

Orchids do not like to sit in excess water. Following the recommended care instructions helps preserve the plant and maintain a healthy appearance.

Maintain a Stable Indoor Climate

Air conditioning, dry heat, and sudden temperature shifts can affect orchids. Keeping the arrangement in a stable environment helps it thrive longer.
