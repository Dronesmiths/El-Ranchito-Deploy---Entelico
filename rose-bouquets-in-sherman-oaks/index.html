Rose Care Tips for Longer-Lasting Beauty

Once your roses arrive, a little care can help them stay fresh longer, especially in Sherman Oaks where indoor temperatures may still run warm during the summer months. Keep the bouquet away from direct sunlight, heat vents, and ripening fruit. Change the water regularly if the flowers are placed in a vase, and trim stems at an angle to improve water absorption.

If your bouquet is being displayed in a home near the foothills or in an apartment with strong afternoon sun, consider placing it in a cooler part of the room. The Valley climate can be unforgiving, but with proper care, quality roses can remain beautiful for days.
