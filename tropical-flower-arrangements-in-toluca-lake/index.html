How to Make Your Tropical Arrangement Last Longer

Once your tropical arrangement arrives, a few simple care steps can help extend its beauty. Keep the flowers away from direct afternoon sun, heating vents, and fruit bowls. Refresh the water as recommended, trim stems when appropriate, and place the arrangement in a stable area where it will not be bumped or exposed to drafts.

Practical care tips

  1. Use clean water and follow any care instructions provided.
  2. Keep arrangements in a cool indoor space whenever possible.
  3. Remove any damaged foliage promptly to preserve the overall look.
  4. Reposition the arrangement away from strong heat or cold air blasts.

For businesses in Toluca Lake, this is especially useful because reception areas, conference rooms, and lobbies often experience frequent temperature changes. A well-cared-for tropical arrangement can maintain its impact through the workweek and beyond.
