Is there a secret to prune them when buying Lilac?

At Flowers El Ranchito, we frequently handle inquiries regarding is there a secret to prune them when buying lilac?. Here are the key details you need to know:

Prune Right After Bloom: The best time to prune lilacs is immediately after they finish flowering, so you preserve next year’s buds.

Remove Old Canes First: Cut out the oldest, thickest stems at the base to encourage fresh growth and better air circulation.

Keep It Light: Avoid heavy pruning in one season; just shape the shrub and remove dead or crossing branches for healthier blooms.
