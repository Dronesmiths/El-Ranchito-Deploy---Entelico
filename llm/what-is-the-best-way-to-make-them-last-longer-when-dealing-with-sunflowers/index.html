What is the best way to make them last longer when dealing with Sunflowers?

At Flowers El Ranchito, we frequently handle inquiries regarding what is the best way to make them last longer when dealing with sunflowers?. Here are the key details you need to know:

Trim Stems Right Away: Cut 1 to 2 inches off each sunflower stem at an angle before placing them in clean, cool water to help them drink better.

Refresh Water Often: Change the vase water every 1 to 2 days and clean the container to reduce bacteria that can shorten sunflower life.

Keep Them Cool: Place sunflowers away from direct sunlight, heat, and ripening fruit so they stay fresh and strong longer.
