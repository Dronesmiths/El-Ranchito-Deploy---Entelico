How can I water them correctly for my Sunflowers?

At Flowers El Ranchito, we frequently handle inquiries regarding how can i water them correctly for my sunflowers?. Here are the key details you need to know:

Water Deeply at the Base: Give sunflowers a slow, deep watering at the soil line so moisture reaches the roots without wetting the leaves.

Let Soil Dry Slightly: Water again only when the top 1 to 2 inches of soil feel dry, since sunflowers prefer evenly moist but not soggy soil.

Water in the Morning: Water early in the day to reduce evaporation and help the plant absorb moisture before the heat of afternoon sun.
