How to Choose the Right Birthday Flowers

A great birthday bouquet starts with a little thought. The best arrangements reflect the recipient’s personality and the setting where the flowers will be displayed. A bouquet for a grandmother in a home near Recreation Park may feel different than a sleek arrangement delivered to an office near the civic center or a family gathering off Glenoaks Boulevard.

Think about color

Bright reds, oranges, yellows, and pinks create an energetic birthday mood. Soft lavenders, blush tones, and whites feel elegant and gentle. If you know the recipient’s favorite color, incorporating it into the bouquet adds a personal touch.

Think about fragrance

Some recipients love fragrant flowers like lilies or garden roses. Others prefer a lighter scent, especially for office deliveries or enclosed spaces. We can help you choose blooms that suit the environment.

Think about the setting

A compact arrangement works well for desks, bedside tables, and restaurant tables. Larger, dramatic designs are ideal for birthday parties, home celebrations, and milestone events.
