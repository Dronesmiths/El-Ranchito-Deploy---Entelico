Tips to help anniversary flowers last longer

  • Place the arrangement away from direct sunlight and heat sources.
  • Refresh the water as recommended.
  • Trim stems lightly if instructed for your bouquet type.
  • Keep flowers away from fruit bowls to reduce ethylene exposure.
