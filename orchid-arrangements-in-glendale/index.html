How to Care for Your Orchid Arrangement

Keep It Out of Direct Sun

Orchids prefer bright, indirect light. In Glendale homes with large windows or west-facing exposure, it is best to keep arrangements away from intense afternoon sun to protect the blooms.

Water Carefully

Overwatering is one of the most common mistakes with orchids. Depending on the arrangement style, watering needs may vary. We provide care guidance so you know how to maintain the flowers without stressing the roots or stems.

Maintain a Stable Environment

Orchids do best in stable conditions, away from heating vents, cooking steam, and frequent temperature swings. This makes placement important in Glendale homes and offices where air conditioning, kitchen activity, or direct drafts may affect longevity.
