Rose Care Tips for Longer-Lasting Blooms

Tarzana’s warm, dry conditions can shorten vase life if roses are not properly cared for after delivery. Fortunately, a few simple steps can help preserve freshness and beauty for days longer.

Best practices after delivery

  1. Trim stems at an angle before placing roses in water.
  2. Use a clean vase filled with fresh, cool water.
  3. Remove leaves that fall below the waterline.
  4. Keep the bouquet away from direct sunlight and heat vents.
  5. Refresh the water regularly and re-trim stems as needed.

Because Valley heat can be intense, especially in summer, it’s smart to place roses in a cooler indoor space rather than by a bright window. That’s especially helpful in homes and offices throughout Tarzana where afternoon temperatures can climb quickly.
