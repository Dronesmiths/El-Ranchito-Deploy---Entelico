Key qualities to look for

  • Freshness: Blooms should be handled carefully and arranged close to pickup or delivery time.
  • Comfort: Wrist corsages should be lightweight and secure without pinching or slipping.

  • Personalization: Ribbon, accent greenery, and bloom style should reflect the wearer’s taste.

Can you match a corsage to a specific dress color?

Yes. Matching colors is one of the most important parts of prom floral design. We can help coordinate tones to complement dresses, suits, and accessories.

What is better for prom: wrist corsage or pin-on corsage?
