How to Extend the Life of a Rose Bouquet

Once the roses arrive, a few simple steps can help preserve their beauty. Proper care can make a noticeable difference, especially during warm Chatsworth afternoons.

  • Trim stems at an angle before placing them in water.
  • Use a clean vase with fresh, cool water.
  • Remove leaves below the waterline.
  • Keep the bouquet away from direct sunlight and heat vents.
  • Refresh the water regularly for best results.

For households near open, sun-exposed streets or upper-level apartments where indoor temperatures can rise quickly, these steps are especially important. A well-cared-for bouquet will retain its shape, fragrance, and color longer.
