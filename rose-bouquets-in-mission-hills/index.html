How to Keep Rose Bouquets Looking Fresh Longer

When your rose bouquet arrives, a few simple care steps can extend its beauty. These are especially helpful in Mission Hills, where warm interiors and dry weather can shorten vase life if flowers aren’t cared for properly.

  1. Trim the stems at an angle before placing them in water.
  2. Use a clean vase with fresh, room-temperature water.
  3. Remove any leaves below the waterline.
  4. Keep the bouquet away from direct sun, vents, and fruit bowls.
  5. Refresh the water every couple of days.

If the bouquet is displayed in a home near the canyon edges or in a room that gets strong afternoon light, moving it slightly out of direct exposure can make a noticeable difference. Roses are resilient, but a little care goes a long way.
