Styles of Prom Corsages We Create

Prom corsages are not one-size-fits-all. Some students want something soft and romantic. Others want a bold statement that coordinates with a sleek gown or suit. We help you choose a style that suits the outfit, the personality, and the overall look of the evening.

Classic wrist corsages

Wrist corsages remain a favorite for prom because they’re elegant, easy to wear, and photograph beautifully. We often design these with roses, spray roses, orchids, carnations, ranunculus, or accent blooms depending on the season and the desired look. Ribbons, greenery, and decorative bands can be matched to dress colors or kept neutral for a more timeless feel.

Pin-on corsages

For students who prefer a traditional presentation, pin-on corsages offer a polished, formal look. These are often chosen to complement tuxedos, jackets, or suit lapels. We construct them carefully so they sit well, stay secure, and remain comfortable throughout the evening.

Modern minimalist designs

Some prom styles call for less volume and more refined simplicity. A single elegant bloom, a delicate cluster, or a sleek monochromatic design can create a high-fashion effect. These are especially popular when the outfit already has strong details such as sequins, satin, metallic accents, or bold tailoring.

Frequently Asked Questions About Prom Corsages in Tarzana

When should I order a prom corsage?

Ordering at least one to two weeks ahead is ideal, especially during peak prom season. Early ordering gives you better flower options and helps secure your preferred style and color palette.

Should a corsage match the dress exactly?

Not necessarily. Some of the best designs either complement the dress or create elegant contrast. We can help you decide whether matching, coordinating, or accenting is the best approach.

What flowers work best for prom corsages?

Popular choices include roses, spray roses, orchids, carnations, ranunculus, and seasonal accent flowers. The best choice depends on the look you want, the available colors, and how durable you need the corsage to be.

Do you offer delivery in Tarzana?

Yes. We can help with delivery options for homes and businesses throughout Tarzana and nearby areas, making it easier to stay on schedule for prom day.

How do I keep the corsage fresh before the event?

Keep it in a cool place, away from direct sunlight and heat. If possible, avoid leaving it in a parked car. Handle it gently and put it on as close to the event as you can.
