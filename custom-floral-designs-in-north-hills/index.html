Our Custom Floral Design Process

We believe the best floral designs come from listening first. Every project begins with a conversation about your preferences, timeline, budget, and intended use. From there, we shape a design concept that balances beauty, freshness, and durability.

1. Consultation and Vision Mapping

We start by learning what you need. That may include event details, color preferences, flower favorites, delivery timing, and any style references you love. If you’re not sure what you want, we can guide you through options based on mood, season, and setting.

2. Flower Selection

Not every flower performs the same way in North Hills conditions. Valley heat, dry indoor air, and long display windows can affect longevity. We select blooms based on both appearance and resilience, often incorporating focal flowers, supportive blooms, and greenery that keep the arrangement looking fresh throughout the day.

3. Design and Assembly

Once the concept is approved, we build the arrangement with careful attention to line, texture, movement, and balance. This is where design becomes art. Whether the style is airy and organic or structured and dramatic, each element is placed with purpose.

4. Delivery and Placement

For local deliveries in North Hills, timing and handling matter. We coordinate deliveries to homes, offices, churches, venues, and retail spaces with care so the flowers arrive in peak condition and are placed exactly where they will have the most impact.
