How long do rose bouquets stay fresh?

With proper care, rose bouquets can remain fresh for several days or longer. Keeping them in clean water, away from direct heat, and trimmed at the stems helps extend vase life.

Yes. Color selection is one of the most important parts of customizing a bouquet, and it is often the easiest way to match the emotion or purpose of the gift.
