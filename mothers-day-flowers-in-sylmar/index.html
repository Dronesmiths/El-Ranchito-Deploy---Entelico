How to Choose the Right Mother’s Day Flowers

Picking the perfect arrangement is easier when you think about the recipient’s personality, home style, and favorite colors. The goal is to make the gift feel tailored rather than generic.

  1. Choose her favorite colors: If she loves soft pinks, choose pastel roses and peonies-inspired arrangements. If she prefers bold tones, go with vibrant oranges, reds, and purples.
  2. Think about her style: Traditional mothers often enjoy classic round bouquets, while modern tastes may lean toward sleek vase arrangements or orchids.
  3. Consider where the flowers will be displayed: A kitchen table arrangement may work best in a compact vase, while a living room centerpiece can be larger and more dramatic.
  4. Match the occasion: A first Mother’s Day may call for something tender and sentimental, while a grandmother might appreciate a fuller, more elegant design.
  5. Plan for delivery timing: If the bouquet is being sent to a home in Sylmar or a workplace near major commuter routes, schedule delivery early to ensure the surprise lands perfectly.
